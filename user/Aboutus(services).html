About Us




Institute of Engineering & Science, IPS Academy

IES IPS is one of the most reputed colleges of Indore. Within fifty one years of it's establishment, it is among the city's peak educational institutions.Other than engineering it offers courses from pharmacy to literature and fine arts to management.



Affiliation& Ranking

It ranks among the top 45 private engineering institutions in India. It offers a 4-year undergraduate program in all engineering courses.The academy is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya Bhopal and is also accredited from National Board of Accredition.
